About me

I'm a second-year Business Information Systems student at Tampere University of Applied Sciences (TAMK), and I'm most at home across the whole stack. I like taking a vague idea and turning it into something that works, from the interface to the API to the database.

For example, CampusEats, a school project, started as the question “where do I eat today?” and ended up as a deployed app that pulls together lunch menus from every Tampere campus into one view. Projects like that, where an idea goes through building to shipping, are exactly what I enjoy most.

I'm currently looking for an internship or junior full stack role where I can learn in a team and work on real production software.
